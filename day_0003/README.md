## day_3 (second_task) (#restaurant bill splitter)

Going out with friends is fun, but calculating how much everyone owes can be confusing, especially when adding a tip for the restaurant staff. 

To solve this everyday problem, I built a **Restaurant Bill Splitter** in Python. This tool automatically calculates the tip amount based on a percentage, updates the total bill, and divides the final amount equally among the number of people present.
you just have to enter the food bill, percentage of tip given to the waiter and the number of person visited to the restaurant.

In this device, i have used the 'f string' for the first time, i have used comments to specify every variable and the most important, I have made a simple workflow to get the correct result.
